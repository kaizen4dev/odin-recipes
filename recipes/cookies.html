Cookies

Description

This sugar cookie icing dries hard and shiny and the colors stay bright. Choose as many different colorings as you desire to make beautiful decorations on your cookies. This recipe makes enough icing to decorate a dozen sugar cookies.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons milk, or more if needed
  • 2 teaspoons light corn syrup

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to low, about 200 degrees F (95 degrees C).
  2. Stir together confectioners' sugar and milk in a small bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat in corn syrup and almond extract until icing is smooth and glossy. If icing is too thick, add more corn syrup.
  4. Divide into separate bowls, and add food colorings to each to desired intensity.
  5. Dip cookies, or paint them with a brush.
  6. Eat!
